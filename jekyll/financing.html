 Residential

Options:

Active Solar offers a variety of financing options for our residential solar energy customers. We will design your solar system to fit your unique needs and budget.

  • Green Jobs NY Loan: Offered through Energy Finance Solutions, this is a term loan for 5, 10, or 15 years for up to $25,000. This loan allows you to purchase your system over time while taking advantage of Federal and State incentives. It is guaranteed by NYSERDA with a current interest rate of 3.49% APR.
  • Solar Lease: A lease gives you the lowest payment without purchasing the solar energy equipment outright. Active Solar offers residential solar leases. Call us for speak with a financial representative about this option.

Incentives:

Based on current Federal and State incentives, you may be able to save up to 2/3 of your project cost. After speaking with your project manager at Active Solar, we may identify that one or more the following incentives will be applicable to your project. Incentives and grants will be applied for on your behalf, during Phase 3 of Our Process

  • NYSERDA Solar PV Grant: NYSERDA currently offers $1.00/watt installed for systems up to 25kW
  • NYSERDA Solar Thermal Grant: NYSERDA currently offers $1.50/kWh of your anticipated yearly system production up to $4000
  • Federal Residential Renewable Energy Tax Credit: This is a 30% tax credit on eligible systems with no limit on size
  • NY State Residential Solar Tax Credit: NYS Residents may be eligible to receive a tax credit of 25% of the system cost up to $5000
  • NY State Property Tax Exemption: Energy Conservation Improvements for single family homes
  • Sales Tax Free: 100% exemption from New York state sales tax on all of your solar projects
* Incentives subject to change based on state programs. Each state has specific solar incentives; call us for program incentives outside of New York State.

 Commercial

Options:

Active Solar can help guide you through the hurdles of financing your new commercial solar system. Whether you own a large corporation or a small farm we can build a system and find a financing option to meet your needs

  • Solar Lease: A solar lease allows your company a low monthly payment with the opportunity to buy out the system at the end of the lease term. The leasing company will utilize the tax credits and depreciation. Give us a call so we can discuss our solar lease options.
  • Power Purchase Agreement (PPA): An Active Solar Development PPA allows you to enjoy energy savings without making the investment in equipment. Active Solar provides the equipment and installation for your solar energy system at no charge. In turn, you will agree to buy power from Active Solar for a 20 year term at a fixed price. The price is discounted from your current utility price to ensure savings.

Incentives:

  • Federal Business Investment Tax Credit:
  • NYSERDA Solar Thermal Incentive Program: NYSERDA currently offers $1.50/kWh of your anticipated yearly system production up to $25,000 for commercial systems
  • NYSERDA Solar PV Grant: NYSERDA currently offers $1.00/watt installed for systems up to 50kW; above 50kW (up to 200kW) the incentive is $.60/watt
* Incentives subject to change based on state programs. Each state has specific solar incentives; call us for program incentives outside of New York State.
