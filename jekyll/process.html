How it Works

Phase 1 | Site Visit

Active Solar Development breaks its projects into three phases. Phase one involves customer relationship building, and the planning and design of the project. a free site evaluation at your desired location, followed by a proposal for the system that will best suit your needs.

  • On-site visit
  • Current energy profile assessment
  • Evaluation of your up-to-date energy bill to calculate your current energy consumption
  • Predict potential future savings

Phase 2 | Proposal

Once you feel comfortable with your proposal we shift to phase two. Phase two is the build phase where we pick out all of the necessary materials for you system and our qualified engineers put the pieces together.

  • Solar energy proposal to meet your specific energy needs
  • Polysun (solar modeling) report to ensure that energy estimates are accurate
  • Accurate payback estimate
  • Financing includes all available incentives and tax credits

Phase 3 | Build

Once the system is finally put together we enter phase three. Phase three involves routine maintenance and commissioning to ensure your system is performing to satisfaction.

  • Our engineers design your solar energy system
  • Our structural engineer will evaluate your roof if necessary
  • State incentives are applied for on your behalf
  • Installation begins upon approval of incentives and permits
  • Interconnection to the grid after installation is complete
  • Final inspection
