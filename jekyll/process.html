 Phase 1 | Site Evaluation

Active Solar Development breaks its projects into three phases. Phase One involves customer relationship building and a free site evaluation at your desired location.

You can expect:
  • On-site visit including solar energy potetial which will answer these questions:
    • Do you have a south facing or near-south facing roof ?
    • Is your roof unobstructed and not shaded?
    • Is your property free from deed restrictions on solar-panel systems?
    • Is your roof in good condition?
    • Do you have open land that may be suitable for a ground mounted system?
  • Evaluation of your up-to-date energy bill to calculate your current energy consumption
  • Development of initial site design and solar model
  • Joint decision is made to move forward to Phase Two

 Phase 2 | Planning and Design

Phase Two is the development of the project including a multi-page proposal for the system that will best suit your specific energy needs and budget.

You can expect:
  • Our engineers will design your solar energy system
  • Polysun (solar modeling) report will ensure that our energy estimates are accurate
  • Accurate payback estimate is calculated
  • Financing options include all available incentives and tax credits

 Phase 3 | Installation and Commissioning

Once the system design is complete we enter Phase Three. Phase Three involves the preparation, build, and commissioning of your solar energy system. Our qualified engineers and installers assemble the components to complete your system.

You can expect:
  • Contract for work is presented and payment schedule is set
  • State and/or Federal incentives are applied for on your behalf
  • Our structural engineer will evaluate your site
  • Building permits are applied for on your behalf
  • Interconnection paperwork is applied for on your behalf
  • Installation begins upon approval of incentives and permits, and receipt of materials
  • Electrical inspection is completed
  • Interconnection to the grid after installation is completed and approved
  • Utility inspection is completed
  • System is commissioned
  • Watch your energy bills shrink!
