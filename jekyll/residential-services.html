Solar Electric(PV)

Tired of opening your electric bill only to find out the rate has increased again? Choosing to install photovoltaic solar panels allows you to replace energy from your utility provider with energy from the sun. Doing so allows you to know your costs for the life of your solar energy system. These systems can be integrated into the utility grid (grid tied) or stand alone systems (off grid). Active Solar Development’s staff can perform a site visit to assess your home’s solar resource and design a system to suit your lifestyle. Contact us to schedule a site visit and start saving!

Solar Hot Water

Did you know that up to 30% of your utility bill may be from an electric water heater? A solar hot water system can provide up to 80% of the energy needed to heat your homes hot water supply. If you have a low temperature heating system like radiant floors we can also help supplement you heating needs. Our expert designers use Polysun solar modeling software so that we can pinpoint the best solar hot water design to suit your needs and budget. Contact us to schedule a site visit.
