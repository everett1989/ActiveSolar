 About Us

Active Solar Development specializes in the design, construction, financing and maintenance of solar thermal and photovoltaic solar systems. We also are a registered NYSERDA PV and Thermal solar installer in NY with NABCEP certified installers on staff. Active Solar Development’s team members have collaborated on the design, manufacture and installation of some of the largest and most efficient solar thermal systems in use today. We have the staff and expertise to offer complete turnkey solar systems to our customers.
