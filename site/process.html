Our Process

Phase 1

Active Solar Development breaks its projects into three phases. Phase one involves customer relationship building, and the planning and design of the project. a free site evaluation at your desired location, followed by a proposal for the system that will best suit your needs.

Phase 2

Once you feel comfortable with your proposal we shift to phase two. Phase two is the build phase where we pick out all of the necessary materials for you system and our qualified engineers put the pieces together.

Phase 3

Once the system is finally put together we enter phase three. Phase three involves routine maintenance and commissioning to ensure your system is performing to satisfaction.
